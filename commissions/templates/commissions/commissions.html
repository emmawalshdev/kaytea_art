Pet Commissions

{% for category in all_categories %} {{ category.friendly_name }} {% endfor %} Pet Commissions

I create commissions of a wide range of animals and wildlife in a variety of media and canvas sizes. Studying photographs as a reference, I use pencil, watercolor, or acrylic; to hand-created each piece.

All work takes place in my West Cork Studio. Using only the finest quality canvas, commissions can be created in various sizes, from A5 up to A1. The details of each commission vary according to both the subject & the client's budget, all of which can be discussed throughout.

Whether it's a cat, dog, horse, or goldfish; I aim to capture every animal's personality in every piece.

The Process

Here's how the commission process works:

  1. Get in touch, by filling in the form below.
  2. Once a request has been made, I will be in touch to chat about your requirements. At this time, we can discuss all available options.
  3. Based on your preferences, I will confirm the total cost and approximate timescale.
  4. Once the quote is accepted, the artwork will commence.
  5. When the Artwork is complete, I will send you photographs. If you are happy with the piece, I will request payment and the painting is yours.
